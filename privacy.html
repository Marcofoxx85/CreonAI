1. Introduction

Welcome to CreonAI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit or use our website, mobile app, and AI-powered tools (“Services”).

By using CreonAI, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.
